How it Works - main section

1. Tell us what you need

We want to learn what makes your business successful. We want to understand your company culture, values, role requirements, and the typical behaviours of your star performers. We’ll efficiently capture this information to start sourcing you top candidates straightaway.

2. Sit back, while we do
the searching

With the help of our matching technology, we’ll get to work scouring a range of online and offline channels, as well as our own database, to surface the perfect candidates for you. We look beyond education and experience, and instead focus on core competencies and behaviours likely to indicate success.

3. We’ll bring the top
talent to you

After we’ve sourced and matched the candidates, they’ll be added to your Applicant Tracking System (ATS) ready for you to carry out a seamless candidate experience. From here, we’ll work with you to refine your talent selection. With each hire, we gather feedback to continually iterate and improve our matches to bring you high quality candidates faster.
